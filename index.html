About Me

Hi! I'm Pragya Neupane. I am a graduate student at Massachusetts Institute of Technology pursuing a dual Master’s in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, and Technology and Policy Program. I am also working as a graduate researcher where my work focuses on using Natural language Processing and Large Language Models in healthcare. I am interested in the intersection of AI and Society.

I also like to call myself a passionate advocate for inclusivity in technology. I have been involved with Nepali Women+ in Computing as an advisor since 2020. Through NWIC, I hope to contribute to a diverse and inclusive tech work force. I love to read and write poetry, listen to Sufi music, play board games, and travel in my spare time.
